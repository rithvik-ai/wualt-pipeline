Detection States
Three-State Model
Clear escalation path with persistence requirements and emergency bypass.
✅
Normal
All vitals within personal baseline or only weak signals (temp alone).
flags.count == 0
OR (flags.temp && count == 1)
"Everything looks steady. You're doing great."
⚡
Stress
Single primary signal deviating. Must persist ≥60s before alerting.
flags.count == 1
AND signal != temp_only
AND persistence >= 60s
"Your heart rate is a bit higher than usual. If you're not exercising, try to relax."
🚨
Distress
Two or more primary signals deviating. Emergency signals bypass persistence.
flags.count >= 2
AND persistence >= 60s

BYPASS: SpO2 < 90%
BYPASS: HR > 150 at rest
"We're noticing signs of distress. If you're not feeling safe, please reach out to someone you trust."
Signal Weights
Four-Channel Fusion
Weighted scoring with physiologically appropriate thresholds. Temperature is deliberately weak — supporting only.
❤️ Heart Rate 0.35
Primary stress indicator. Elevated resting HR strongly correlates with sympathetic activation.
z > +2.0 abs > 120 bpm emerg > 150 bpm
💫 SpO2 0.30
Critical safety signal. Desaturation below 94% triggers clinical concern; below 90% is a medical emergency.
z < -2.0 abs < 94% emerg < 90%
🌊 HRV (RMSSD) 0.25
Autonomic nervous system marker. Suppressed HRV indicates reduced parasympathetic tone — chronic stress signature.
z < -1.5 inverted ↓
🌡️ Skin Temp 0.10
Weak supporting signal. Skin ≠ core temperature. Elevated temp alone never triggers stress — only supports other signals.
z > +2.5 abs > 37.8°C advisory only
